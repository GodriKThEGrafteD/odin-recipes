Kashmiri Pulao

by, Sanjay(hope you guys like it)

Description :

Indulge in the aromatic allure of Kashmiri Pulao, a culinary masterpiece that captures the essence of the enchanting Kashmir Valley. Each spoonful is a journey through fragrant saffron-infused basmati rice adorned with vibrant, jewel-like dried fruits and aromatic spices. Immerse yourself in the symphony of flavors as you encounter the delicate sweetness of plump raisins and the subtle crunch of slivered almonds, all harmonizing to create a taste that is both luxurious and comforting. The pulao is further elevated with the presence of tender, caramelized onions and the earthy richness of Kashmiri spices. Kashmiri Pulao is not just a dish; it's a celebration of Kashmir's rich culinary heritage. As you savor each bite, let the unique blend of sweet and savory, along with the visual feast of colorful ingredients, transport you to the serene landscapes of Kashmir. An irresistible delicacy that brings warmth and joy to your table, making every meal a memorable experience.

Ingredients :

  • Saffron-infused Basmati Rice
  • Dried Fruits (Raisins, Apricots)
  • Slivered Almonds
  • Caramelized Onions
  • Kashmiri Spices (Cloves, Cinnamon, Cardamom)
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Vegetables (Optional)
  • Water
  • Salt to taste

Steps :

  1. Rinse Basmati rice thoroughly and soak in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and sauté Kashmiri spices until fragrant.
  3. Add caramelized onions, dried fruits, and slivered almonds. Sauté until golden.
  4. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pan. Stir gently to coat the rice witSh spices.
  5. Pour in water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is cooked.
  6. Fluff the pulao with a fork, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve hot.
